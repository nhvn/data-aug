About AugmentAI

AugmentAI is a project designed to provide advanced data augmentation and synthetic image generation using Generative AI models such as GANs. Developed for the HackAI - Dell and NVIDIA Challenge, the project demonstrates the integration of NVIDIA AI Workbench for efficient model training and deployment.
